References

You must reference any information used in your report or in the creation of your artefact.
You will probably need 10 - 20 links that you used placed in here.

You must reference and acknowledge all research sources used such as:
publications including books, professional journals and government reports; online sources and other types of media; source code;
any material generated using artificial intelligence (AI) software or applications; and material from specialist organisations and relevant individuals.
To include such material without properly referencing the source will be considered plagiarism.
In addition, the copying from, or reproduction of, material from such sources may also be considered plagiarism.
